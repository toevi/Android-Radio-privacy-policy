Privacy Policy – Radio Android

Last updated: November 2025

Radio Android is a free internet radio application. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

1. Personal Data Collection

Radio Android does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users.

The app does not require account creation, login, or access to personal information.

2. Data Used by the App

Radio Android:

  • does not collect analytics data
  • does not use tracking technologies
  • does not collect device identifiers
  • does not access location
  • does not request unnecessary permissions

Only basic technical data handled by Android (such as network connection state) may be processed automatically by the system, but the app itself does not collect or store it.

3. Advertising

Radio Android does not display any advertisements and does not use third-party ad networks.

4. Purchases and Payments

The app contains only an optional external link to BuyCoffee (Buy Me a Coffee–type donation site).

Radio Android:

  • does not include in-app purchases,
  • does not process any payments inside the app,
  • does not access payment information.

Any donation through BuyCoffee is handled entirely by the external service according to its own privacy policy.

5. Streaming Content

The app only plays free internet radio stream URLs provided by public radio stations. No user data is collected during playback.

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any external SDKs, analytics tools, or trackers.

Following the external BuyCoffee link is optional and handled outside the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Radio Android does not target children and does not collect any data, in compliance with Google Play requirements.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the app functionalities change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
